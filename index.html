How to Play

Single Payer Pong is a game that tests your skills against the PC.

Control the bottom paddle using the left and right arrow keys.

The objective of the game is to get the ball behind the AI controlled paddle to score a point.

The first player to get to 10 wins!

Welcome to PONG

Please note this game has been designed to be played on desktop exclusively.
It will require the use of a keyboard.
